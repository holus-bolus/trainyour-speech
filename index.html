Service Description

Individual online presentation skills essentials training. We will cover the following topics:

  1. Essential Presentation Techniques

    In this module, we will explore the visual, vocal, and verbal components of a successful presentation. You will learn easy-to-memorize tips and tricks to be used when presenting.

  2. Methods of engaging your audience

    In this module, we will explore some tips for engaging and managing your audience

  3. How to present online

    There are some nuances when presenting online, which we will gladly share with you. Together we will explore the importance of voice, visuals, and technology during an online presentation. We will also briefly explore commonly used tools for online presentations

  4. Three minutes before you go on the stage?

    We will share with you some ways to minimize the fear of public speaking considering the uniqueness of your personality and style and will give you some exercises for practicing your presentation before going on the stage

  5. Common mistakes

    We will teach you some common mistakes which you will try to avoid during your presentation

  6. Q&A
